02医疗
各级医疗卫生机构：medical and health institutions at various levels\r\n一线医护人员：front-line medical workers\r\n口罩：(face) mask\r\n戴上、取下和处理口罩：put on, take off and dispose of a mask\r\n防护服：protective gowns /clothing /gear /suits\r\n护目镜：goggles\r\n一次性手套：disposable gloves\r\n医疗物资：medical supplies\r\n疫苗：vaccine\r\n自我隔离：quarantine oneself\r\n监测体温：monitor/check body temperature\r\n自觉接受医学观察：voluntarily put oneself under medical observation\r\n解除医学观察：be discharged from medical observation\r\n诊断、治疗、追踪和筛查：diagnosis, treatment, tracing and screening

03防护
早发现、早隔离：\r\nearly detection and early isolation\r\n\r\n公共场所消毒、通风以及体温检测：\r\ndisinfection, ventilation and body temperature monitoring in public areas.\r\n\r\n用肥皂和清水或含有酒精的洗手液勤洗手：\r\nFrequently clean hands by using alcohol-based hand rub or soap and water.\r\n\r\n咳嗽和打喷嚏时，屈肘或者用纸巾遮掩口鼻：\r\nWhen coughing and sneezing, cover mouth and nose with flexed elbow or tissue.
